# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** April 15, 2026
**Last updated:** April 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how OZAL ("we," "us," "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the OZAL mobile application (the "App"). OZAL is operated by Justin Lee, an individual based in New York, United States.

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at **privacy@ozal.golf**.

---

## 1. Who this applies to

OZAL is intended for users **18 years of age and older** who reside in the **United States**.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If you believe someone under 18 has provided us with personal information, contact privacy@ozal.golf and we will delete it.

OZAL is currently available only in the United States. We do not market the App to users outside the U.S.

---

## 2. Information we collect

We collect the following categories of information:

**Account information**
- Email address (used for authentication)
- Display name and username
- Password (stored in encrypted form by our authentication provider)
- Profile photo (if you choose to upload one)

**Practice content you create**
- Practice videos ("clips") you record or upload
- Voice notes you record (private to your account; not shared with other users)
- Practice metadata: club type, location type (Range, Simulator, or Home), facility name, swing notes, captions, and dates
- AI-generated transcripts and summaries of your voice notes

**Location information**
- When you tap "Find nearby facilities" while logging a session, we request your device's approximate location through iOS in order to search for golf facilities near you. Your coordinates are sent to Google Places for the search and are **not stored** on our servers. Location access is requested only when you use this feature, never in the background, and never on app startup.

**Device and usage information**
- Push notification token (used to deliver notifications to your device)
- Product analytics events (screens viewed, features used, taps, session duration) collected through PostHog
- Basic device information (iOS version, device model)

**Limited security logs**
- If our servers receive an invalid or malformed location request (for example, coordinates outside the valid range), we log the event for abuse-detection purposes. These logs do not contain real user location data.

We do **not** collect: payment information, phone numbers, dates of birth, contacts, health or fitness data, social media account information, or background location.

---

## 3. How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

- Provide and operate the App, including authentication, content storage, and playback
- Generate AI summaries of your voice notes (see Section 4 for how this works)
- Display your shared content (clips submitted to challenges or appearing on leaderboards) to other users where you have chosen to share
- Send you push notifications related to your practice activity, crews, and challenges
- Search for golf facilities near you when you use the "Find nearby facilities" feature
- Understand how the App is used so we can improve it
- Detect, prevent, and respond to abuse, fraud, and security incidents
- Comply with legal obligations

We do **not** sell your personal information. We do not use your content to train AI models, and our third-party AI provider (OpenAI) does not use your content to train its models when accessed through its API.

---

## 4. How we share your information

We share your information only with the third-party service providers we use to operate the App. We do not sell your data to advertisers or other third parties.

| Service | What we share | Purpose | Location |
|---|---|---|---|
| Supabase | All account data, practice metadata, profile photos | Backend database, authentication, image storage | United States |
| Mux | Practice video clips | Video hosting and streaming | United States |
| Amazon Web Services (S3) | Voice notes | Audio storage | United States |
| PostHog | Usage analytics events | Product analytics | United States |
| Google Places API | Approximate location at moment of search | Nearby facility lookup | Governed by Google's privacy policy |
| OpenAI | Voice note transcripts | AI summary generation (Whisper transcription + GPT-4o-mini summarization) | Governed by OpenAI's API privacy terms |
| Apple Push Notification Service | Push notification tokens and messages | Push notification delivery | Governed by Apple's privacy policy |

Other users of OZAL can see content you choose to share publicly within the App, including:
- Your display name, username, and profile photo
- Clips you submit to challenges
- Your appearance on leaderboards

Voice notes are **private to your account** and are not shared with other users, including members of your crew.

We may also share information if required by law (such as in response to a valid subpoena or court order), or to protect the rights, safety, or property of OZAL, our users, or the public.

---

## 5. Data retention

- **Active accounts:** We retain your account data and practice content for as long as your account is active.
- **Deleted accounts:** When you delete your account through the in-app deletion flow, we remove your data from Supabase, Mux, and AWS S3, and we delete your authentication record, within **30 days**.
- **Analytics data:** PostHog retains aggregated analytics events according to its standard retention policy.
- **Security logs:** Abuse-detection logs are retained for a reasonable period to detect patterns of misuse.

---

## 6. Your rights and choices

**Access and correction.** You can view and update most of your account information directly in the App through your profile settings.

**Account deletion.** You can delete your account at any time through the in-app account deletion flow in Settings. This permanently removes your videos, voice notes, profile photo, and account data from our systems within 30 days.

**Data export.** If you would like a copy of your data, email **privacy@ozal.golf** and we will provide it within a reasonable time frame.

**Push notifications.** You can disable push notifications at any time through your device settings.

**Location access.** You can revoke location permission at any time through your device settings. The "Find nearby facilities" feature will simply stop working; the rest of the App will continue to function.

## 7. California residents

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) gives you additional rights:

- **Right to know** what personal information we have collected about you
- **Right to delete** your personal information
- **Right to correct** inaccurate personal information
- **Right to opt out of "sale" or "sharing"** of your personal information — we do not sell or share your personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising
- **Right to non-discrimination** for exercising these rights

To exercise any of these rights, contact **privacy@ozal.golf**. We will respond within 45 days.

## 9. Security

We use industry-standard practices to protect your information, including:

- Encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) for all data transmission
- Server-side authentication and access controls
- Pre-signed, time-limited URLs for video and audio access
- EXIF metadata stripping on uploaded profile photos
- Server-side input validation and rate limiting

No method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but we work continuously to protect your information.
